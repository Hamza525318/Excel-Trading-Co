    5 Fastener Types Driving India's EV & Infrastructure Boom

    What Buyers & Manufacturers Need to Know in 2025

    Home › Blog › India EV Fasteners Market 2025
    Industry Insight By Excel Trading Corporation · 5–6 min read · EV · Automotive · Infrastructure

    India's fasteners market is entering a high-growth phase. Driven by electric vehicles, large-scale infrastructure expansion and domestic manufacturing mandates, the market reached approximately USD 11.2 billion in 2025 and is projected to grow to nearly USD 17 billion by 2034 — at a steady CAGR of around 4.5–5%.

    The biggest structural shift? Electric vehicles. As EV production scales rapidly across India, the demand for lightweight, high-strength and corrosion-resistant fasteners is increasing in lockstep — and it is not a temporary spike. It is a permanent change in what the market needs.

    For OEMs, suppliers and procurement teams, this growth is not just about volume — it is a shift in which types of fasteners are in demand and why. Here are the five categories seeing the strongest traction in India's EV and infrastructure ecosystem right now.

    1 Lightweight Aluminium & High-Strength Fasteners

    Weight reduction is not optional in EV design — it is a core engineering objective. Every kilogram saved improves battery efficiency and extends vehicle range. This has accelerated the adoption of aluminium fasteners and high-strength steel fasteners (Grade 10.9 and above) in EV platforms, where both approaches achieve weight reduction without sacrificing joint integrity.

    ⚡

    Why This Category Is Growing

    Indian EV manufacturers are under increasing pressure to extend range without enlarging battery packs. Switching from standard Grade 8.8 steel to Grade 10.9 or aluminium allows smaller-diameter fasteners at the same load — directly reducing mass across the full assembly.

    Grade 10.9 Aluminium Fasteners M6 – M12

    Typical Applications: Battery enclosures, motor housings, structural brackets, chassis sub-assemblies

    Industry Insight: High-strength flange bolts (DIN 6921 Grade 10.9) are increasingly specified in EV battery pack structural frames — the integrated flange eliminates the need for a separate washer, reducing part count and assembly time on high-volume production lines.

    2 SS316 & Coated Fasteners for Corrosion Resistance

    India's climate — coastal humidity in Chennai, Mumbai and Kochi, monsoon exposure across Karnataka and Maharashtra, and high-temperature industrial environments in Punjab and Gujarat — makes corrosion resistance a genuine engineering requirement, not a premium upgrade.

    🌊

    Why SS316 Is Gaining Ground Over SS304

    SS304 (A2) handles most outdoor environments well. But EV battery enclosures and underbody assemblies face concentrated road spray, cleaning agents and salt exposure that degrade zinc plating over a vehicle's 8–10 year service life. SS316 (A4) adds molybdenum to the alloy — giving it significantly better resistance to chloride attack, making it the specification of choice for external battery pack fasteners in coastal and high-humidity markets.

    SS304 / A2 SS316 / A4 EV Battery Packs Solar Structures

    Why it matters: Corrosion-related fastener failure in battery mounts does not just cause a structural problem — it can breach the battery enclosure seal and introduce moisture into the pack. SS316 is a lifecycle investment, not a luxury specification.

    Typical Applications: EV battery pack external mounting, solar panel racking, outdoor telecom enclosures, marine and coastal structures

    3 High-Precision Fasteners for Automated Assembly

    India's automotive sector is moving rapidly toward robotic and automated assembly — driven by both domestic OEM investment and multinational supplier requirements. Automated assembly systems cannot tolerate dimensional variability in fasteners. A screw that is 0.2mm off-pitch will jam an automated feeder and stop a production line.

    🤖

    What Automation-Ready Means in Practice

    High-precision fasteners for automated assembly are manufactured to tighter thread tolerances (typically 6g or better), consistent head height and surface finish, and tight length tolerances — ensuring reliable feeding, driving and torque performance every cycle. In EV battery module assembly especially, torque consistency is safety-critical: under-tightened cell compression fasteners affect thermal contact; over-tightened ones can crack module components.

    6g Thread Tolerance M6 – M20 Automotive EV Battery

    Key Requirement: Consistent threading, head height and torque performance across every batch — not just the first sample

    4 Fasteners for Renewable Energy & EV Infrastructure

    The EV boom is not just about vehicles — it extends to charging infrastructure, solar energy systems and smart city construction. These applications require fasteners that can withstand outdoor conditions across a 20–25 year service life with minimal maintenance.

    ☀️

    The Infrastructure Fastener Requirement

    Solar panel mounting systems demand Grade 8.8 or higher tensile strength combined with hot-dip galvanising or stainless steel finish for 25-year outdoor durability. EV charging station structural fasteners face similar requirements. Both applications are seeing volume demand growth that is independent of vehicle sales — infrastructure installation in India is accelerating regardless of EV adoption rate.

    Grade 8.8+ Hot-Dip Galvanised SS304 / SS316 Solar EV Charging

    Growth Driver: India's solar capacity target of 500 GW by 2030 alone represents tens of millions of fastened structural connections. Every mounting frame, racking system and inverter enclosure requires fasteners specified for outdoor service life.

    5 Self Clinching & Specialty Fasteners for Thin Sheet

    EV battery enclosures, server chassis, electronics housings and switchboard panels are all built from thin sheet metal — typically 1.0mm to 2.5mm gauge. This thin material cannot be tapped to create threads, and standard nuts require access to both sides of the panel. Self clinching fasteners solve both problems in a single operation.

    🔩

    Why Clinching Fasteners Are Growing in EV Applications

    A self clinching nut or standoff is pressed into a pre-punched hole in a thin sheet metal panel — creating a permanent, flush, reusable thread without welding, heat or adhesive. In EV battery enclosure manufacturing, they enable clean, precise assembly of thin aluminium and steel panels that cannot be conventionally tapped. In electronics and switchboard manufacturing, they are the standard solution for mounting PCBs, DIN rails and cover plates.

    Clinch Nuts M3–M8 Clinch Standoffs Clinch Studs EV Enclosures Electronics

    Typical Applications: EV battery enclosures, server chassis, switchboard panels, telecom rack enclosures, electronics housings, PCB mounting

    What This Means for Buyers & Manufacturers

    The shift toward EVs and infrastructure is not just increasing fastener demand — it is changing the specification requirements. Buyers are no longer selecting purely on price. Performance, weight, corrosion resistance and compatibility with automated assembly systems are all becoming primary criteria.

    Key Takeaways for Procurement Teams

    • Upgrade from Grade 8.8 to Grade 10.9 where weight reduction matters — smaller bolts, same load capacity
    • Specify SS316 (not just SS304) for EV underbody and external battery pack connections in coastal and high-humidity markets
    • Ensure fastener dimensional tolerances meet your assembly system's feed and drive requirements before placing bulk orders
    • Switch to self clinching fasteners for thin sheet metal assemblies — eliminates welded studs, loose nuts and backing plates
    • Plan for solar and EV infrastructure fasteners with a 20–25 year service life specification — not a 5-year procurement cycle

    For manufacturers and fabricators, the implication is equally clear: the teams that align their fastener sourcing strategy with India's EV and infrastructure requirements today will be better positioned for the next decade of growth.
