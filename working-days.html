Same-Day Dispatch Cut-off

Urgent Enquiries

Message us on WhatsApp outside business hours and we'll respond when we reopen.

How This Affects Delivery

Delivery timelines are counted in working days, not calendar days. An order dispatched on Saturday will typically be delivered on the following Monday or Tuesday, since transport partners also observe Sunday closures.
